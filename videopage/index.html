The Hoodoo Method

We have conceived a systematic approach that helps startups to get their product right. Our Product Doctors are equipped with the Hoodoo package and will review every single aspect of your product problem startup and will work with you to solve each of them so you can reach success

  • Product design

    The design of your product is very important for your users. We will work with you to solve your UX/UI problems. Is your design good enough? Are you using the right design patterns?

  • Funnel

    We will make sure your funnel is complete. Are you having On-boarding/Sign up problems?Do you have a problem with your Sign-up? Are your users leaving you platform/app? Do you want to understand what is going on? and how to solve it? We can help you.

  • Market

    Is your product satisfying the market? Are you targeting the right Market?Does your product have a real market? Is your solution what the customer wants? Let us work with you to find that out.

  • Strategy

    Knowing where your product is heading is essential. How are you dealing with competition? Is your product vision the right one for growth? Does your product need to take a new path in order to reach succcess?

  • Customers

    Do you understand what your customers really want? Have you iterated enough and have you pivoted in the right direction?

  • Tech

    Are you building your product with the right technology? Are you really ready to scale? Do you know what it takes to scale?

OUR FAQ

We know that we are an innovative startup and we know you might have some urgent questions so we have assembled the onces that seem to be the most requested ones. Do not hesitate to share with us the other questions!

HOW CAN I BE SURE MY PROJECT IS KEPT CONFIDENTIAL?

We only show your project’s details to the product managers that work on it and these Product managers are under a strict NDA with us. Every precaution is taken to keep our systems secure.

HOW DO YOU PUT TOGETHER A PROPOSAL SO QUICKLY? IS THIS SERIOUS?

Our product managers are real and our work 100% guaranteed. If we guess wrong and lose money on your project, that’s on us. We’re able to quote so quickly because we’ve seen enough projects that, after a short conversation, we can tell with a high degree of certainty what almost anything should cost.

HOW GOOD ARE YOUR PRODUCT MANAGERS?

We go to great lengths to find and recruit the best product experts in the world. Hoodoo was actually designed from the products’s perspective first - as a concierge service to find the best product experts interesting work.Since we offer the best product experience in the world we are able to recruit the best in the market. They come from top schools like MIT, Stanford, and Caltech and tech titans like Google, Facebook, and Pinterest. Many are startup founders too.

EXACTLY WHICH PRODUCT MANAGERS ARE GOING TO WORK ON MY PROJECT?

We can’t tell you in advance because we don’t match your project with product managers until it starts and we have the first call. Once we know what project are you working on we will match you with the best PM we have for you. The match is a labor-intensive process - we want to assemble the perfect team - and we wouldn’t want to show our product experts a bunch of NDA-protected projects they might not be working on, so we don’t perform a match until clients make their first payment and we know that the project is really happening.

YOU SOUND TOO GOOD TO BE TRUE. HOW CAN YOU DO IT THIS CHEAP?

Agencies and development shops have a lot of overhead and are fundamentally lifestyle business, meaning that their focus is profits. Hoodoo has the mission to reduce the amount of failed startups and therefor we want to be accessible all the startups that need that help. We would rather earn a referral from you than take a huge margin!
